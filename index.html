Research

My research is aimed towards Trustworthy, Ethical, and Equitable AI Augmenting Human and Societal Potential.

Within this broad vision, I have several ongoing research thrusts:

  1. Human-State-Aware & Trustworthy AI
  2. Ethical & Equitable ML
  3. AI for Culture, Nature & Society
